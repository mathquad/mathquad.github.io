This reseach involves:
  • Explanation of solutions to a given math problem by generating a sequence of solution micro-steps, (execution of all such micro-steps would yield the correct solution).
  • Determination of math problem micro-steps for the entire K-12 curriculum.
  • Comprehending information provided in text and images (understanding of problems that encode part of the information in companion images).
  • Explanation of the solution in an interactive dialog format, taking into cosideration the prior knowledge (in the curriculum) of the learner.

The research effort will address the following challenges:
  • A master and hieractical map of math concepts and associated problem solving micro-steps.
  • Understand information encoded in both text and images (of math problems).
  • An authoring enviroment where experts can write detailed and step-by-step solutions to math problems - utilizing the math-micro-steps and adding additional meta data that can help train the neural net.
