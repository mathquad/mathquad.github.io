Math Question Understanding and Answering Dataset


Contains a comprehensive set of questions that span the gamut of elementary, middle, and typical high school math curriculum for testing the ability of an automated bot or dialog system to answer questions related to the solution of such math problems.
The curriclum provides a map what a student is supposed to learn. Based on where a student is in the learning process, the bot / dialog system can utilize pre-requisites to formulate the dialogs.
Solutions steps + where a student is to develop a dialog.
