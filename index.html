Math Question Understanding and Answering Dataset


This dataset contains K-12 curriculum based "psets" for training neural nets and bots for automated explanation of strategies for solving math problems. The problems in the PSETS contain detailed step by step strategies for solving the given problem.
The focus is not on providing the right answer, but on explaning how to solve the given problem.
The use of the curriculm map enables the automated system to detect and monitor the progress of a learner and the system can adjust and adapt its dialog and explanation to cater to the needs of the learner.
